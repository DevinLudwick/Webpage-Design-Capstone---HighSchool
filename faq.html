FAQ

What is the highest price for one of your suits?
Our suits max out at $300.00, we do not charge over that!

What is the average price for one of your suits?
Our suits are typically sold around $220 - $280, but we have many options that are lower/higher.

What sizes are the suits sold in?
Our offered suit sizes are as follows: 38R, 40R, 42R, 44R, 46R, 48R, 50R. We plan on offering more sizes in the near future!

Where can I find information on returning damaged items?
Click the menu-icon in the top-left corner (3 stacked lines), then, click on "About", then, click on "Returns."

How do I find out about current sales?
On the main page of the website, we have a slideshow set up that shows all current sales on the store!
